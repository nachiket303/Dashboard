The person behind the models

I am a Data Scientist with 4+ years building production ML systems in banking, insurance and fintech. I have shipped models real businesses depend on, not just notebooks that live in GitHub.

My work spans the full pipeline: understanding the business problem first, engineering features from messy multi-source data, training and validating models correctly, deploying them reliably, and making sure the outputs are explainable enough for compliance teams and advisors to genuinely trust.
